Project : GamerConnect

  • Source code

This project involves developing GamerConnect, a platform for gamers to connect, share content, and organize gaming events. The platform includes features such as user profiles, messaging, and event management. It is built using React, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB.

GamerConnect aims to create a community for gamers where they can easily find and connect with other gamers, share their gaming experiences, and organize or participate in gaming events. The platform enhances the social experience of gaming.

  • Utilizes Django,SQL and HTML,CSS
  • Includes features such as user profiles, messaging, and event management
  • Creates a community for gamers to connect and share content
